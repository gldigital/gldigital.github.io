How To Play

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game. There are 4 crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number chosen. You lose the game if your score goes above the random number chosen. Each time a new game starts, each crystal will have a different value.
